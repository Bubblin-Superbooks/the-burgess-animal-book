Chapter Xxiii.

Digger And His Cousin Glutton

“Well, Peter,” said Old Mother Nature, “did you visit Digger the Badger yesterday?”

“Yes'm,” replied Peter, “I visited him, but I didn't find out much. He's a regular old grouch. He isn't the least bit neighborly. It took me a long time to find him. He has more holes than anybody I ever knew, and I couldn't tell which one is his home. When I did find him, he gave me a terrible scare.
